Violist Hannah Leigh has performed as an orchestral and chamber musician throughout the United States, Europe, and South America. Hannah has appeared as the principal violist of the Frost Symphony Orchestra and the Peabody Symphony Orchestra in recent seasons, and recorded under the Naxos label with Leonard Slatkin and acclaimed flautist Marina Piccinini. She completed undergraduate studies with Victoria Chiang at Peabody Conservatory and a Master's degree under the instruction of Jodi Levitz at the Frost School of Music.
